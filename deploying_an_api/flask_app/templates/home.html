Recommender System

First you have to train the recommender by clicking here: Train

You can also send your reset data with a POST request to the route "/train". You need to provide the parameters nb_users, nb_items, user_history, item_history, rating_history.

To use the recommender just write the user_id and item_id in the fields below, or use the route "/predict?user_id=XX&item_id=XX"
